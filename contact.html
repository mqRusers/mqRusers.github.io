--- layout: post permalink: /contact title: Contact Us cover: assets/images/dawn.png --- ## Frequently asked questions
Do I need to have experience coding in R to attend an R Users Group meeting?
No! Everyone is welcome to attend, but you may find it easier to follow along with our presenters if you have at least a passing familiarity with R and RStudio.
How do I find out when your next meeting is?
Please check our Upcoming Events here. We have a mailing list that we send notification of upcoming meetings to, please email us to be added to the list to receive these notifications.
I can't make it to an upcoming meeting or I missed a previous meeting. Can I still access the materials?
Each presentation has it's own repository where you can find the materials. If the meeting was an online meeting you can find a link the recording in the repository.
